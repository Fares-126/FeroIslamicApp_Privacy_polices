Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 11, 2026

Welcome to Fero Islamic App. Your privacy is our priority. This policy outlines our commitment to your data security and explains how we handle information.

The Short Version: Fero Islamic App does not collect, store, or share any of your personal information. We do not require any device permissions to function.

1. Information Collection

Fero Islamic App is built with a "Privacy by Design" philosophy.

  • Personal Data: We do not collect any personal identification information (such as name, email address, or phone number).
  • Usage Data: We do not track how you use the app, your prayer times, or your "Fero Sebha" counts.
  • Location Data: We do not track your GPS location.

2. Permissions

This application does not require any sensitive permissions (such as access to your camera, contacts, microphone, or storage) to provide its core features. All calculations and functions are performed locally on your device.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not use third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks that track your behavior or collect data from your device.

4. Data Security

Since no data is collected or transmitted to our servers, there is no risk of your personal data being compromised from our side. Any data created within the app (like your local settings) stays strictly on your physical device.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
